Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 18, 2025

Important: This Privacy Policy explains how ScanWith Phone ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Scanned Documents: Text and images from documents you scan using the app
  • Document Titles and Tags: Names and categories you assign to your documents
  • App Settings: Your preferences for scan quality, color mode, and other app configurations
  • Purchase Information: Records of in-app purchases processed through Apple's App Store

1.2 Information Automatically Collected

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version
  • Usage Analytics: App crashes, performance data, and feature usage (via Google Mobile Ads SDK)
  • Advertising Data: Anonymous advertising identifiers and ad interaction data
  • Biometric Data: Face ID/Touch ID authentication data (stored locally on your device only)

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Core App Functionality

  • Process and store your scanned documents
  • Perform OCR (Optical Character Recognition) to extract text
  • Sync your documents across your devices via iCloud
  • Enable document search and organization features
  • Secure your documents with biometric authentication

2.2 App Improvement

  • Analyze app usage to improve features and performance
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Develop new features based on user needs

2.3 Advertising (Free Version)

  • Display relevant advertisements through Google Mobile Ads
  • Provide rewarded ads for earning additional scans
  • Measure ad performance and effectiveness

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

  • Documents and images are primarily stored on your device
  • Biometric authentication data never leaves your device
  • App settings and preferences are stored locally

3.2 iCloud Storage

  • Documents are synced to your personal iCloud account
  • Only you can access your iCloud data with your Apple ID
  • We do not have access to your iCloud-stored documents
  • iCloud data is encrypted both in transit and at rest

3.3 Security Measures

  • End-to-end encryption for iCloud synchronization
  • Optional biometric authentication (Face ID/Touch ID/PIN)
  • Secure communication protocols (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Regular security updates and patches

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Google Mobile Ads

We use Google Mobile Ads to display advertisements in the free version of our app. Google may collect:

  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA/GAID)
  • Device information and IP address
  • App usage and ad interaction data
  • Location data (if permitted by you)

Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4.2 Apple Services

  • iCloud: Document synchronization and backup
  • App Store: In-app purchase processing
  • StoreKit: Purchase verification and management

Apple's privacy policy: https://www.apple.com/privacy/

5. Your Privacy Rights

5.1 Data Access and Control

  • Access: You can view all your documents within the app
  • Export: Export your documents in PDF or other formats
  • Delete: Remove individual documents or clear all data
  • Settings: Control scan quality, color mode, and sync preferences

5.2 Advertising Preferences

  • Limit ad tracking in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Apple Advertising
  • Reset advertising identifier in iOS Settings
  • Purchase the ad-free version to remove all advertisements

5.3 Data Deletion

  • Delete the app to remove all local data
  • Manage iCloud data in Settings > Apple ID > iCloud > Manage Storage
  • Contact us for assistance with data deletion requests

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

7. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than your own, including the United States, where our servers and third-party services are located. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with applicable privacy laws.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last updated" date
  • Providing in-app notifications for significant changes

9. Data Retention

  • Documents: Stored until you delete them or uninstall the app
  • Analytics Data: Retained for up to 2 years for app improvement purposes
  • Advertising Data: Managed by Google according to their retention policies
  • Purchase Records: Retained as required by Apple for transaction history

10. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Union, our legal basis for processing your data includes:

  • Consent: For advertising and optional features
  • Contract Performance: To provide the app's core functionality
  • Legitimate Interest: For app improvement and security
